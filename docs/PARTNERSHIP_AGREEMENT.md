### 2. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY (IP)

### 3. REVENUE SHARE & COMPENSATION
The Technical Partner is entitled to **25% of the Net Profit** generated by the project. 
- **Marketing Fund:** 12% of Gross Revenue shall be deducted and reinvested into user acquisition before profit calculation.
- **Operating Costs:** Hosting, payment gateway fees, and legal costs are deducted to reach the Net Profit.
- **Example:** $10k Revenue - $1.2k Marketing - $0.8k Costs = $8k Net Profit. CTO Share = $2k.

### 4. ROLES & RESPONSIBILITIES
- **Technical Partner:** Responsible for backend architecture, security (Bank/Ledger), multiplayer synchronization, and technical maintenance.
- **Founder:** Responsible for business strategy, game design vision, marketing, and legal compliance.

### 5. PROJECT STATUS & LEGALITY
The parties agree to develop "Depth Clash" as a **Skill-based / Cyber Sport** platform. Both parties will ensure the project remains compliant with international laws regarding digital assets.

### 6. TERMINATION & CONTINUITY
This agreement remains in effect as long as the Technical Partner actively maintains and improves the project. If the Technical Partner ceases activity for more than 30 consecutive days without prior notice, this agreement may be terminated by the Founder.
